Bluetooth® Connection Assistant

Troubleshooting

We're sorry you're having problems. If your device is still not connected, or if it keeps disconnecting from time to time, please try the following:

  • Check that there is sufficient charge in the batteries.
    If you are using a rechargeable device, you can place the device in the rapid charger until the LED indicator on the device is green.

  • If a USB mini-receiver was supplied with your device, close this dialog and the Bluetooth® Connection Assistant, then plug in the mini-receiver. Then open the Bluetooth® Connection Assistant again and follow the Troubleshoot Connection instructions to connect your devices with the receiver.
    If you are not sure whether a receiver was supplied, you can check the package content of your device.

  • If your device is provided with no USB mini-receiver, you may refer to your computer manufacturer's documentation to find how to manage the integrated Bluetooth® radio.
