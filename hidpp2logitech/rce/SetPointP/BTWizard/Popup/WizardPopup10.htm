Bluetooth® Connection Assistant

Bluetooth® Device Not Detected

The Bluetooth® Connection Assistant could not detect the integrated Bluetooth® radio on your computer.

Please re-enable your integrated Bluetooth® radio. You may refer to your computer manufacturer's documentation to find how to manage your integrated in Bluetooth® radio. In many cases, this is controlled by a switch or an Fn key on your keyboard (notebook).



If you are unable to turn on the integrated Bluetooth® radio, you can connect your devices with the Bluetooth® receiver by clicking on the "Use Bluetooth® Receiver" button below and follow the on-screen instructions.
