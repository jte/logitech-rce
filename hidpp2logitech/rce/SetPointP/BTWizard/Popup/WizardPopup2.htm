Troubleshooting

We're sorry you're having problems. If your device is still not connected, or if it keeps disconnecting from time to time, please try the following:

  • Check that there is sufficient charge in the batteries.
    If you are using a rechargeable device, you can place the device in the rapid charger until the LED indicator on the device is green.

  • Make sure the receiver is properly connected to a USB port.

  • Try plugging in the receiver to another USB port on the computer.

  • Connect the receiver to a USB extension cable if your devices become disconnected from time to time.
    For optimal performance place the receiver at least 8 inches or 20 centimeters away from other electrical devices, such as the computer and the monitor.

  • Recommended. Turn off the Bluetooth® hub functionality:

    Your Bluetooth® devices will be connected directly to your receiver, but other Bluetooth® devices will be disconnected.

    Click the Disable Hub button below.

Your device should now be connected properly.
