Illumination On Screen Display Setting


SetPoint can display the level of illumination intensity when you manually increase or decrease illumination.

You can dim or brighten illumination on your keyboard by pressing the FN key and either F5 or F6 at the same time. There are five levels of brightness ranging from full illumination to no illumination.

By pressing the FN key plus the F6 key, illumination will increase

When you press the FN key plus F5, your keyboard's illumination will decrease.

When you select, "Display visual notification when I manually adjust illumination intensity", SetPoint will briefly display a message on your screen indicating the level of illumination intensity.
