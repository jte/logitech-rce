Low Battery Notification

Low Battery Notifications appear briefly on your screen once every three days for a total of nine days. We suggest that you change the batteries on your device during this period.

If you want to, you can disable this feature in the Tools tab by unchecking the Low Battery Notification check box.

Note: Devices with long battery life (greater than 1 year) may continue to work for a few weeks or longer after the Low Battery Notification is displayed.
