Software Access

The SetPoint™ software contains a range of settings that you can adjust, including notification settings.

Check the Show SetPoint icon in notification area box if you want the SetPoint icon to be displayed in your Windows® taskbar (usually near to your computer's clock).

We want you to use the most up-to-date version of the SetPoint software to ensure you are making the most of your product and have access to the latest features. If you want to update your software to the latest version, click Check now.

To adjust the software update settings, click Configure. You can then choose whether or not to either allow SetPoint to periodically check for software updates.
