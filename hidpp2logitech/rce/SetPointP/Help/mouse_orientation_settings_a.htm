Orientation Settings

Depending on how you use your trackball, rolling the ball "up" may result in the pointer moving at a different angle than desired. Use the Set Orientation feature to align the ball motion with the pointer motion.

Click the Set Orientation button and roll the trackball forward, in the direction you want to be "up." As you roll the trackball, the black ball should move up the picture. When it reaches the top, the process is complete and the orientation should be set. Click Apply to save the settings.

Note: If you did not roll the trackball in a straight line, you will be prompted to retry the process.
