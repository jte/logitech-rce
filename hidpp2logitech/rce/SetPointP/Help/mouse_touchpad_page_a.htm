TouchDisc Scroll Settings

Use these sliders to adjust how you want your keyboard's integrated TouchDisc to work.

Drag one of the 'scrolling' sliders to select your vertical or horizontal scroll speed. The faster the speed, the less accurate the scrolling.

Drag one of the 'acceleration' sliders to choose your vertical or horizontal scrolling acceleration - low acceleration for accuracy or high acceleration for increased speed scrolling. Using accelerated scrolling means the scrolling gets faster the longer you scroll.
