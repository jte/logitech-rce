Scroll panel settings

You can adjust the scroll settings of your mouse's touch pad to suit the way you want to use it.

Drag the scrolling slider to select your scroll speed. The faster the speed, the less accurate the scrolling.

Drag the acceleration slider to choose your vertical scrolling acceleration - low acceleration for accuracy or high acceleration for increased speed scrolling. Using accelerated scrolling means the scrolling gets faster the longer you scroll.

If you want the scrolling to continue even after you remove your finger from the scroll panel, and then eventually slow down, check the Allow inertia scrolling box. With this box unchecked scrolling stops immediately when you release your finger.
