Game Settings

Use your mouse for gaming? Here's where you can define settings to maximize your gaming potential.

Checking the Game detection box instructs the SetPoint™ software to detect games and enables the other mouse game mode settings.

Use the Game mode settings check boxes to specify your mouse's gaming settings. They include whether you want your mouse's pointer speed and acceleration when gaming to be the same as with other applications, and whether button assignments defined in SetPoint remain in use when gaming.
