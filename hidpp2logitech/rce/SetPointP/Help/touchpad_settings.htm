Touchpad Settings

Do more with your Logitech Touchpad than just point and click. Choose the functions you want by checking the box next to each item.

NOTE: Your Logitech Touchpad may not support all functions. Only the configurable options are displayed in the tab.

  • Pointer Movement: Uncheck this function if you want to disable pointer control and use your touchpad only for gestures.

  • Tap-to-Click: Tap the touchpad to perform a left-click.

  • Move Left/Right to Scroll Horizontally: Drag two fingers side-to-side to scroll horizontally.

  • Move Up/Down to Scroll Vertically: Drag two fingers up or down to scroll vertically.

  • Scroll with Inertia: Flick two fingers to continue scrolling after your fingers leave the touchpad. The faster you flick your fingers, the farther you will scroll.

  • Tap with 2 fingers to right-click: Tap the touchpad with two fingers to perform a right-click.

  • Spread or pinch with 2 fingers to zoom: This option will only work in applications that support zooming.

  • Swipe from left edge to switch applications: Swipe from left-to-right across the left edge of the touchpad with one finger to switch applications in Windows 8.

  • Swipe from right edge to show Charms: Swipe from right-to-left across the right edge of the touchpad with one finger to show the Charms in Windows 8.

  • Swipe from top edge to show App Bar: Swipe downward across the top edge of the touchpad with one finger to show the App Bar in Windows 8 applications.

  • Swipe Left/Right for Browser Back/Forward: Swipe three fingers side-to-side to go forward or backward thru web pages.

  • Swipe Up/Down for Page Up/Down: Swipe three fingers up or down to move up or down a page.

  • Swipe Left/Right for Windows Snap: Swipe four fingers left or right to snap the active window to the left or right side of your screen.

  • Swipe Up/Down to Switch Applications and Show Desktop: Swipe four fingers up to see all your open windows, then tap or click on the window you want to bring into view. Swipe four fingers down to minimize or restore all open windows.
